International Chemistry Bowl

OUR MISSION

The goal of our staff members at ICB is to provide an environment where high-school students can express their academic interests and excellence in the field of chemistry through various multiphase and virtual tournaments: all of which feature cash prizes and are free of cost to participate. Being completely student-run, ICB strives to stimulate collaboration and curiosity among the next generation of STEM leaders.
